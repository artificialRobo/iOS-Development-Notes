## Introduction to the Advanced Section

### Purpose of the Advanced Section

* Covers more challenging aspects of **Swift programming** and **iOS development**.
* Focuses on concepts that go beyond basic app creation.

### Difference from Previous Sections

* **Beginner & Intermediate Sections:**

  * Teach how to build apps.
  * Focus on adding features and creating projects.
* **Advanced Section:**

  * Focuses on becoming a better programmer.
  * Helps create apps that are:

    * More **robust**
    * More **reliable**
    * More **scalable**

### Prerequisites

* Complete the **Beginner** and **Intermediate** sections first.
* Ensure all previously learned concepts are well understood before continuing.

### Keep in Mind

* The advanced modules are designed to improve programming skills and software quality, not just teach app-building techniques.
