## Notes: Introduction to Mobile App Design

### Course Introduction

* Presented by **Angela** from the **London App Brewery**.
* The course is an introduction to the **art of mobile app design**.

### Target Audience

* Designed for **app entrepreneurs** and aspiring app creators.
* Intended for people who:

  * Have app ideas or want to start an app company.
  * Have **little or no formal background in design**.
  * Want to understand the fundamentals of app design quickly.

### Why the Course Was Created

* The development studio worked with many clients who wanted to build apps.
* Many people have app ideas but lack a strong understanding of **design principles**.
* The course was created to provide an accessible overview of essential app design concepts.

### Topics Covered

The course combines key concepts from:

* **Graphic Design**
* **Digital Design**
* **User Interface (UI) Design**
* **User Experience (UX) Design**
* All focused specifically on **mobile app design**.

### Course Philosophy: Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule)

* Angela is a strong believer in the **Pareto Principle**:

  * **80% of results come from 20% of the most important inputs.**
* The course focuses on the **20% of design knowledge** that delivers **80% of the benefits**.
* Goal: Help learners create **beautiful, emotional, and lovable app designs** efficiently.

### What the Course Is Not

* Not intended to train learners for careers as professional **UI/UX designers**.
* Not a comprehensive design degree or certification.

### Main Goal

* Provide a **fast-track introduction** to the most important and rewarding aspects of app design.
* Help beginners gain practical design knowledge without needing formal training.

## Key Takeaway

This course is a beginner-friendly, fast-track guide to mobile app design that teaches the most impactful design principles from graphic design, UI, and UX, helping non-designers create attractive and user-friendly apps.
