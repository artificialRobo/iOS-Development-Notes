# Notes: Icon Design Differences (iOS vs Android)

## 1. Icon Style

**iOS**

* Since **iOS 7**, Apple has adopted **flat design** principles.
* Icons are:

  * **Simple and minimalistic**
  * **Line-based (outlined)**
  * Use **limited gradients, shadows, and 3D effects**

**Android**

* Icons are generally:

  * **Bolder and blockier**
  * More solid in appearance than iOS icons

---

## 2. Design Considerations

* When selecting or creating icons, follow the design style of the target platform.
* If working with a graphic designer, specify whether the icons are for **iOS** or **Android** to ensure they match platform guidelines.

---

## **Key Takeaways**

* **iOS:** Uses **flat, line-style icons** with minimal visual effects.
* **Android:** Uses **blockier, solid-style icons**.
* Matching icon styles to each platform helps create a consistent and professional user experience.
