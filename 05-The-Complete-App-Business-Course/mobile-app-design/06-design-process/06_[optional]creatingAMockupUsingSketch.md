## Optional: Creating a Mockup Using Sketch

## Overview

* This is an **optional, hands-on module**.
* It demonstrates the **step-by-step process of creating a mockup from scratch** using Sketch.
* To follow the guided walkthrough, you need to watch the tutorial video included in the course.

---

## Key Instructions

* Watch the Sketch tutorial if you want a guided demonstration.
* The tutorial covers the complete workflow for building a mockup in Sketch.

---

## Learning Options

### Option 1: Watch the Tutorial

* Follow a **45-minute guided walkthrough**.
* Learn the design process step by step.
* Faster way to become familiar with Sketch.

### Option 2: Learn by Practice

* Skip the tutorial and experiment on your own.
* Expect to encounter challenges while figuring things out.
* Self-discovery helps reinforce learning and improves long-term retention.

---

## Advice

* There is **no single correct approach**.
* If you prefer structured learning, watch the tutorial.
* If you learn best through experimentation, practice independently.
* Although self-practice may take longer, it often results in a deeper understanding and better memory of the process.
