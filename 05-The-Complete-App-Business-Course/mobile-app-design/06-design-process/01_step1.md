# Notes: Design Patterns and Color Palettes

## Project Goal

Design a simple workout app with **3 main features**:

* **Choose workouts** to perform.
* **Track workouts** and view progress/stats.
* **Workout guidance screen** to guide users through exercises.

---

## Step 1: Find Design Inspiration

Before designing, explore existing apps to understand good design patterns.

**Recommended websites:**

* Mobile Patterns
* Pttrns

**Purpose:**

* Browse curated app designs by category.
* Learn from successful UI designs.
* Gather ideas for layouts, navigation, and interactions.

**Key Idea:**

* Inspiration helps generate design ideas instead of starting from scratch.

### Example Inspiration

Inspired by the **Sunrise Calendar** app.

Possible layout:

* **Top navigation bar** for easy navigation.
* **Action buttons** (e.g., Start Workout, Back).
* **Calendar view** showing workout days with colored circles.
* **Detailed workout information** at the bottom (e.g., workout duration, training time).

---

## Step 2: Choose a Color Palette

A consistent color palette creates an attractive and professional design.

**Options:**

* Create your own palette (ideal for graphic designers).
* Use pre-made palettes (easier for beginners).

**Recommended tool:**

* Color Hunt

**Benefits:**

* Browse professionally designed color palettes.
* Copy hexadecimal (hex) color codes directly into design projects.
* Search palettes by **Popular** or **Hot**.

### Choosing Colors for the Workout App

Desired mood:

* Fun
* Vibrant
* Energetic

**Tip:**

* Save the selected **hex color codes** in a note-taking app for easy reference during the design process.

---

## Key Takeaways

* Start by defining the app's core features.
* Gather inspiration from successful app designs.
* Adapt good design patterns instead of copying them exactly.
* Choose a consistent color palette that matches the app's purpose.
* Keep design resources (like color hex codes) organized for future use.
