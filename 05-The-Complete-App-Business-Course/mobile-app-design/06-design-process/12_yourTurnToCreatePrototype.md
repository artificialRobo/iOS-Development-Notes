# Notes: Creating Your App Prototype

## Objective

* It's now your turn to create a prototype for your app.
* Continue working on the **recipe app** you've been designing.

### Progress So Far

You should already have:

* A **flow diagram**
* **Wireframes**
* **Mockups**

### Next Step

* Build an interactive **prototype** using either:

  * **Keynote**
  * **Marvel**

### Why Use Marvel?

* Create interactive prototypes.
* Test prototypes on a real phone using the free **Marvel** app.
* Available for both **Android** and **iOS**.
* Log into your account to access and demonstrate your prototype.

### Additional Resources

* Review the instructor's **[workout app prototype](https://marvelapp.com/314j414)** for inspiration while building your recipe app.

### Share Your Work

* If you're proud of your design, share your prototype link in the course comments for a chance to be featured.

### Need Help?

* Leave a comment if you have any questions or need clarification.

## Key Takeaway

The design process progresses as:
**Flow Diagram -> Wireframes -> Mockups -> Interactive Prototype**
