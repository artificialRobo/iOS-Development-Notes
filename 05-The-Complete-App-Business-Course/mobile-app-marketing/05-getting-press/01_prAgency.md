# Notes: Do I need a PR agency?

## Key Question: Do You Need a PR Firm?

* Many founders wonder whether they should hire a PR firm to get media coverage.
* PR firms can provide valuable exposure, but they are often expensive (around **$100/hour**).

### Advice for Startups

* If you're a **small company or startup**, hiring a PR firm is usually **not necessary**.
* Many **tech journalists prefer hearing directly from founders** rather than receiving pitches from PR agencies.
* Founder-led outreach can be **just as effective—or even more effective—than using a PR firm**.

---

## When a PR Firm Might Make Sense

* Consider hiring one if:

  * You have **significant marketing budget**, or
  * Your startup is **growing (around 20+ employees)** and can justify the cost.

---

## Main Takeaway

* **Pitch your story yourself as the founder** instead of hiring a PR firm in the early stages.
* Save the expense until your company has grown enough to benefit from professional PR support.
