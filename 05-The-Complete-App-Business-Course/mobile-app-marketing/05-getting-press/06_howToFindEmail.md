# Notes: Finding Contact Information for Journalists

## Next Step After Identifying Journalists

* Once you know **who to approach**, the next step is finding their **contact details**.

## Tool: VoilaNorbert

* **VoilaNorbert** is a tool for finding professional email addresses.
* You provide:

  * First name
  * Last name
  * Company/domain (e.g., TechCrunch.com)
* The tool tests common company email formats (e.g., first.last, initial+surname) to identify the correct email address.

### How It Works

1. Enter the person's first and last name.
2. Enter the company website/domain.
3. The tool checks different email address patterns.
4. It returns the verified email address if found.

### Uses Beyond PR

VoilaNorbert can also help with:

* Contacting journalists
* Finding venture capitalists (VCs)
* Finding angel investors
* B2B sales outreach
* Reaching company executives (e.g., VP of Sales)

### Key Benefit

* Saves time by helping you find the correct professional email instead of searching manually.
* Makes targeted outreach easier and more efficient.

## Key Takeaway

**After identifying the right people to contact, use email-finding tools like VoilaNorbert to locate verified professional email addresses for personalized outreach.**
