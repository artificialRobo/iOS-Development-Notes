## Notes: Building Relationships with Journalists

### Why Relationships Matter

* Building a relationship with journalists **greatly increases** your chances of getting coverage.
* **Cold pitching** (contacting someone with no prior interaction) is much less effective.

### How to Build Relationships

* Follow journalists on **Twitter/X**.
* Learn about:

  * Their interests.
  * The topics they cover.
  * What they are passionate about.
* Engage thoughtfully with their posts.
* Reply with **meaningful, intelligent comments** instead of generic responses.

### Be Recognizable

* Aim to become someone the journalist recognizes.
* Instead of being just another person sending mass emails, become someone who regularly contributes valuable conversations.

### Why Twitter/X is Important

* Most tech journalists have an active Twitter/X account.
* They often share:

  * Personal insights.
  * Industry opinions.
  * News and updates.
  * Content not posted elsewhere.

### Best Practice

* Follow journalists who cover your industry or niche.
* Build genuine, ongoing interactions **before** pitching your story.

## Key Takeaways

* Relationships improve pitching success.
* Avoid relying solely on cold emails.
* Engage consistently and thoughtfully on Twitter/X.
* Become a familiar and trusted name before making your pitch.
