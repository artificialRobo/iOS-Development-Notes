# Notes: Using Google Tools to Validate Interest among people

## 1. Google Trends

**Purpose:** Measure public interest in an idea over time.

**What it does:**

* Shows search interest for a keyword over time.
* Allows comparison of multiple search terms.
* Lets you filter by:

  * Country or worldwide
  * Time period

**How to use it:**

* Enter keywords related to:

  * Your app idea
  * The problem your app solves
  * The benefit your product provides

**Insights you can gain:**

* **Interest over time**

  * Identify whether interest is growing, declining, or seasonal.
  * Helps determine the best time to launch.
* **Interest by region**

  * Discover which countries or regions have the highest demand.
  * Helps with localization (e.g., translating the app).
* **Related topics & searches**

  * See what users are also searching for.
  * Understand user interests and generate feature/content ideas.

**Example:**

* **Pokémon Go**

  * Huge spike in searches around its 2016 release.
  * Interest gradually declined afterward.

## 2. Google Keyword Planner (Google Ads)

**Purpose:** Estimate search demand and competition for keywords.

**Originally designed for:**

* Creating Google Ads.

**Also useful for:**

* Market validation.
* Measuring audience size.
* Assessing competition.

**How to use it:**

* Select **Get search volume data and trends**.
* Enter keywords users would type into Google.

**Key metrics:**

* **Monthly search volume**

  * Indicates how many people search for the keyword.
* **Competition**

  * High competition = many businesses are targeting the keyword.
  * Low competition = fewer competitors, but investigate why.

**Examples:**

* **Hair extensions**

  * 100K–1M monthly searches.
  * High competition.
  * Indicates strong demand but many competitors.
* **Tattoo ideas**

  * 100K–1M monthly searches.
  * Low competition.
  * May represent an opportunity, but consider whether the niche is difficult to monetize.

---

## Important Questions to Ask

* Is there enough demand for my idea?
* Is interest increasing or decreasing?
* Is the market seasonal?
* Which countries should I target?
* Can the niche be monetized?
* Why is competition high or low?

---

## Key Takeaways

* **Google Trends** helps you understand **interest over time**, **regional demand**, and **related searches**.
* **Google Keyword Planner** provides **monthly search volume** and **competition data**.
* Using both tools together helps validate:

  * Market demand
  * Audience size
  * Competition
  * Best launch timing
  * Whether an idea is worth pursuing before building the product.
