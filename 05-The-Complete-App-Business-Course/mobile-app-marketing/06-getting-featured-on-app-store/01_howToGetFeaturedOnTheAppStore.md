# Notes: How to Get Featured on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store

## Why Getting Featured Matters

* Being featured on the homepage of the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store** can be worth an estimated **$300,000 in app marketing value**.
* It provides significant visibility and can greatly increase app downloads.

### Key Factor: Strong Early Performance

App stores pay close attention to an app's performance during its initial launch period, especially:

* **First 24 hours**
* **First 7 days**
* **First 30 days**

Strong download numbers and user engagement during these periods improve the chances of being featured.

### How to Boost Early Performance

Focus on generating downloads immediately after launch by:

* Creating a **landing page** before launch.
* Driving traffic to the landing page.
* Using **blogging/content marketing** to build an audience and fan base.
* Encouraging users to download the app as soon as it becomes available.

---

## Main Takeaway

* Early launch momentum is one of the most important factors for getting featured.
