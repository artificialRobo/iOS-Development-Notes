# Notes: How to Get Featured by Apple & Google

* **Understand their priorities:** Apple and Google promote what benefits **their own company and ecosystem**.
* **Align your app with their goals:** Build features that showcase or enhance their latest products or technologies.
* **Example:** If Apple launches the **Apple Watch**, an app that makes the watch more useful or attractive is more likely to receive promotion.
* **Stay updated:** Follow new features and technologies introduced by both companies.

  * Examples:

    * **Apple:** 3D Touch (historical example)
    * **Google:** Instant Apps (historical example)
* **Be an early adopter:** Apps that quickly integrate new platform features have a better chance of being featured.
* **Leverage their marketing:** If your app supports their latest initiatives, you may benefit from Apple or Google's large marketing reach.
* **Key takeaway:** Continuously monitor what Apple and Google are developing and position your product to fit their current strategic focus.
