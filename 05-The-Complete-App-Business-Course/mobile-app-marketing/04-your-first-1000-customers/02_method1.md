# Notes: Email Marketing for App Launches

## Why Email Marketing Matters

* Email marketing is still one of the most effective marketing channels.
* Despite the belief that email is outdated, **email remains highly valuable** for reaching interested users.

---

## Importance of the App Launch Period

* The **first 24 hours, 7 days, and 30 days** after launch are the most important.
* Both the **iOS App Store** and **Google Play Store** monitor app performance during these periods.

### Key Metrics App Stores Track

* Number of downloads
* Number and quality of reviews
* User engagement (time spent in the app)
* Frequency of app opens
* Other engagement signals

### Why Strong Early Metrics Matter

* App stores use algorithms to identify **trending apps**.
* Strong early performance increases the chance of being **featured**.
* Being featured on the iOS App Store can be worth **approximately $300,000 in marketing value**.

---

## Role of an Email List

* Build an email list **before launching** the app.
* Aim for **10,000+ subscribers** who have voluntarily expressed interest in downloading the app.
* A large, interested audience helps generate strong launch-day downloads and engagement.

### Best Practices for Email Marketing

* **Do not buy email lists** or send unsolicited emails.
* Purchased lists often result in:

  * Low email deliverability (messages go to spam).
  * Damage to your sender reputation.
  * Lower success rates for future legitimate email campaigns.
* Only email people who have **opted in** to receive updates.

A high-quality, permission-based email list is one of the most effective tools for driving a successful app launch, improving early app store metrics, and increasing the likelihood of being featured.
