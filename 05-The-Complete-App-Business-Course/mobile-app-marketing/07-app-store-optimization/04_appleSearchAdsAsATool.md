# Notes: Apple Search Ads Keyword Research Tool

## What is Apple Search Ads?

* Apple introduced **Search Ads**, allowing apps to appear as sponsored results in the App Store.
* Similar to **Google Search Ads**, where paid ads appear at the top of search results.
* Developers can promote their apps for specific keywords, even if the app doesn't rank organically.

### When Search Ads Can Help

* Useful for:

  * New apps with few or no downloads.
  * Apps with little brand recognition.
  * Apps that lack reviews.
* However, testing suggests that **advertising can be expensive** and conversions may cost a lot.

---

## Hidden Benefit: Free Keyword Research

* Visit **searchads.apple.com** and sign in with an Apple ID.
* Create a new Search Ads campaign.
* Access Apple's **keyword research tool** without:

  * Running an ad.
  * Paying Apple.

### Why This Tool is Valuable

* Similar to the **Google Ads Keyword Planner**.
* Shows the **search popularity** of keywords directly from Apple.
* More reliable than third-party tools like **Sensor Tower**, which estimate search volume.

### Example

If you enter the keyword **"coffee"**, Apple may suggest:

* Coffee
* Common misspellings of coffee
* Coffee cafe
* Starbucks

These suggestions reveal what users are actually searching for and can inspire additional keywords to target.

---

## Key Takeaways

* Apple Search Ads isn't only for advertising—it also provides a powerful **free keyword research tool**.
* The keyword data comes directly from Apple, making it highly trustworthy.
* Related search terms (such as brand names like **Starbucks**) can help identify new opportunities to reach relevant users.
* Use the tool to improve **App Store Optimization (ASO)** by targeting popular and relevant keywords.
