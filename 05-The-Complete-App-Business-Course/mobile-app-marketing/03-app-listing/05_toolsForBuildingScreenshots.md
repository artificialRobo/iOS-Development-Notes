# Notes: Creating Professional App Store Screenshots

## Tools for App Screenshot Mockups

* **MockUPhone (mockuphone.com)**

  * Drag and drop app screenshots into phone mockups.
  * Makes the app look like it's being used on a real device.
  * Improves the professional appearance of App Store listings.

* **LaunchKit (launchkit.io)**

  * Free screenshot builder.
  * Upload screenshots and automatically add iPhone or Android device frames.
  * Customize:

    * Background color or image.
    * Text above or below the screenshot.
  * Simple and quick to use.

### Export Features

* Generates screenshots in multiple sizes for:

  * Android devices.
  * iOS devices (different iPhone models, e.g., iPhone 5).
  * Apple Watch.
* Ensures screenshots match the device users are viewing in the app store.

## Best Practice

* **Split test** different screenshot designs.
* Let user data determine which designs perform best and increase conversions.
